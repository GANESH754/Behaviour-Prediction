⚙ How Scoring Works

Every answer option carries hidden weight values across all five behavioral indicators. When you select an answer, those weights are added to your running score for each indicator.

At completion, each indicator's raw score is divided by the maximum possible score for that indicator and expressed as a percentage. The indicator with the highest percentage determines your dominant behavioral archetype.

  • Social Orientation — How much you rely on others for support, decisions, and energy.
  • Decision Style Strength — How structured and deliberate your decision-making is.
  • Learning Adaptability — Your openness to new experiences, feedback, and growth.
  • Risk Handling Balance — How you manage uncertainty, pressure, and the unknown.
  • Consistency Score — Your tendency toward discipline, routine, and follow-through.
